What's the Canter?

Want to blow away those Christmas cobwebs and stretch your running legs, then come and give our 8(ish) mile trail race a go!

Northowram Pumas will once again be hosting the Coley Canter on 29 December 2018. This year the route is slightly different, but don't worry, it's just as good!

This year all the money made from the race will be donated to our charity of the year, Andy's Man Club, #itsokaytotalk
